Is there an open redirect in HTTPSRedirectMiddleware?
No, it builds `https_url = f"https://{host}{full_path}"`. `host` is validated against `allowed_hosts` OR extracted from `Host` header.
Wait, `host = req.get_header("host")`
If there are no allowed_hosts, it blindly redirects. But it only changes scheme from http to https, on the same host. Not open redirect.
